    Project Roadmap¶

    We've got a lot planned for Velora and are interested to see how far Liquid RL can take us.

    RL is a huge field and we can only cover a small sample of it. Our goal is to adapt normal RL algorithms into Liquid ones and have additional extensions for things like Curiosity for custom experiments.

    So far, we have a set of algorithms and extensions in mind with other options to consider. For the remainder of this roadmap we have a breakdown of different sections with checkboxes. Items that are green are already implemented.

    Primary¶

    These are items that are mandatory for the framework to reach a v1 release.

    • Algorithms

      • DDPG - 2015
      • TRPO - 2016
      • A2C - 2016
      • PPO - 2017
      • TD3 - 2018
      • SAC - 2018

    • Extensions

      • Replay Buffer
      • Rollout Buffer
      • Prioritized Experience Replay - 2016
      • Curiosity - 2017
      • HER - 2018
      • Forward-Backward (FB) - 2018
      • Forward Imagination - 2021

    Secondary¶

    These are extra items to consider for the framework beyond v1 release.

    • Algorithms

      • ACER - 2016
      • ACKTR - 2017
      • DSAC - 2019
      • DeepGait - 2020
      • Meta Motivo - 2024
      • GRPO - 2024
      • Langevin SAC - 2025

    • Extensions

      • Meta Learning - 2017
      • Imitation Learning - 2021
      • Hierarchical RL - 2021
      • Reverse Offline Model-based Imagination (ROMI) - 2021
      • Goal Oriented Forward-Backward - 2021

    There are many more items to consider on the OpenAI Spinning-up papers list [] too that we plan to review in the future.
